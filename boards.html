We Having Fun Yet?

The Digital Maker and Fabrication degree at University of Advancing Technology (UAT) focuses on designing and building new technology products using digital fabrication tools. This applied technology degree blends user-centric design, materials selection, embedded systems, and rapid prototyping with hands-on technologies such as 3D printing, laser cutting, and CNC fabrication. Students learn to take ideas from concept through prototype while preparing for careers in product design, digital manufacturing, and technology innovation.
